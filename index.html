About Alex Ayalew

Who I Am

Alex Ayalew Mohammed is the CEO and General Manager of Spark AH Trading PLC, a dynamic company dedicated to technology solutions, professional training, and event management.

With a BSc in Economics and a Banking & Finance Diploma, Alex brings over 20 years of experience across multiple industries, including Banking, Education (General Wenget TVET), Agriculture, and Hospitality.

His leadership philosophy is built on integrity, innovation, and empowerment – values that have guided Spark AH Trading PLC to become a trusted partner for organizations across Ethiopia.

Our Company

Spark AH Trading PLC is a legally registered Ethiopian company established in 2016, providing professional services in technology solutions, capacity building, consultancy, project implementation support, and professional training.

The company focuses on delivering practical, innovative, and impact-driven solutions that help organizations and individuals improve performance, develop digital capacity, and achieve sustainable growth.

Mission: To empower organizations and communities through knowledge, technology, and professional development services.

Vision: To be the leading provider of technology-driven solutions and professional capacity building in East Africa by 2035.
